# SOC 2 (Type 1 / Type 2 Ready) Documentation

## 1) Security
- Firewall management standards
- DDoS protection baseline
- Encryption policy for transit and storage
- Vulnerability scanning cycle
- Penetration testing governance

## 2) Availability
- Uptime SLA target and service commitments
- Monitoring and incident escalation
- Failover strategy and regional resilience
- Auto-scaling policy

## 3) Processing Integrity
- Booking validation controls
- Payment integrity verification controls
- Error logging, alerting, and replay workflow

## 4) Confidentiality
- Data classification policy
- Encryption at rest requirements
- Data masking requirements

## 5) Privacy
- Data collection notices and legal basis
- Consent management controls
- Data retention rules and enforcement
- Data deletion workflow and evidence trail
