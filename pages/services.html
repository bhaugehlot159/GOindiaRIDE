What Customers Should Add While Booking

Complete details help avoid the common problems seen in cab bookings: wrong pickup, unclear timing, vehicle mismatch, and fare confusion.

  • Pickup and drop location with landmark, hotel name, gate, terminal, platform, or full address.
  • Trip type: local, airport, hourly rental, outstation one-way, outstation round trip, or multi-stop plan.
  • Journey date, pickup time, return date where needed, and expected waiting time.
  • Passenger count, luggage count, vehicle preference, route notes, and any senior citizen or accessibility need.
  • Mobile number for confirmation, driver coordination, and support escalation.
Tip: for airport trips, add flight number and terminal. For outstation rides, add stopovers, toll preference, and whether the car should wait or return empty.
