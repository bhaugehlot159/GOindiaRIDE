Cookie Policy

Last updated: 11 June 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how GO India RIDE may use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies on the website, booking form, dashboards, and support pages.

1. What Cookies Do

  • Cookies help a website remember information, keep sessions stable, improve security, and understand how pages are used.
  • Local storage may keep temporary preferences, booking UI state, or non-sensitive workflow details in the browser.
  • Some cookies are essential for website operation; others support analytics, preferences, or performance improvement.

2. Types We May Use

  • Essential cookies: security, session, form continuity, login state, and fraud prevention.
  • Preference cookies: language, theme, layout, device, and saved non-sensitive display choices.
  • Analytics cookies: page usage, performance, errors, service improvement, and booking funnel understanding.
  • Support cookies: help us understand support flow, contact page usage, and issue reporting paths.
  • Third-party cookies: may be set by embedded maps, analytics, payment, communication, or security providers where used.

3. Why We Use Cookies

  • To keep booking, login, driver, customer, and admin workflows stable and secure.
  • To remember user preferences and reduce repeated setup steps.
  • To measure page performance and improve content, navigation, and booking completion.
  • To prevent abuse, detect technical errors, and improve service reliability.

4. User Controls

  • You can block or delete cookies through browser settings.
  • Blocking essential cookies may break login, booking, security, or dashboard functions.
  • You can disable browser location permission separately from cookies.
  • Where a consent banner or preference control is provided, you can update choices through that control.

5. Data Relationship

Cookie-related information may be linked with booking, support, device, or usage data where needed for security, analytics, fraud prevention, or service improvement. See the Privacy Policy for broader data handling details.

6. Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy when website technology, analytics, booking flow, dashboards, or third-party tools change.
