Data Safety Details

These details support Play Store and App Store disclosures for the GO India RIDE web/PWA app surfaces: public booking, customer app, driver app and private admin app.

Sharing And Service Providers

GO India RIDE uses service providers for infrastructure and app functionality. Data may be processed through Firebase, web push services, payment gateways, email/SMS/WhatsApp providers, hosting, analytics/security tools and maps/geocoding providers as needed for the selected feature.

  • Driver GPS is used for assigned ride tracking and admin safety monitoring.
  • Payment data is routed through enabled gateways such as Razorpay, PayPal or UPI-compatible payment flows.
  • Push notifications are sent only after browser/app permission and are limited to ride, account, safety and service updates.

Security Practices

  • Production API traffic is expected over HTTPS with secure backend validation.
  • Push subscriptions store endpoint hashes and encrypted push keys; private VAPID keys are not exposed to the browser.
  • Razorpay payment verification uses server-side signature validation; PayPal payment completion uses backend order capture.
  • Admin operations are separated from public/customer/driver flows and require authenticated access.
  • Users can use the public Account Deletion page or contact support for account, ride, payment, privacy or deletion requests.

Deletion And Support

For data access, correction, deletion, refund or support requests, use the Account Deletion page, the contact page, or email privacy@goindiaride.in. Some transaction, safety, tax, security and legal records may be retained where required by law or legitimate operational obligations.
